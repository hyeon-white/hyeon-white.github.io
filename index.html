Research Experience

Implantable device for Heart Failure Mar 2023 - present

In this project, I am developing implantable SMA device for Acute Heart Failure patients, collaborating with cardiovascular surgeon. Our team conducted dozens of pig tests with cardiovascular sensing equipments. We also experimented in-vivo pig test with developing SMA device. We are ongoing process of writing paper

Haptic feedback device for robot surgeryMar 2024 - present

Collaborating with breast-endocrine surgeon, we are developing haptic feedback system for training surgeons for robot surgery. In the shape of gloves, we are making vrious types of vibration using arrays of vibration motors for different situations.

Haptic feedback shoes for visually impairedJul - Aug 2024

Our team developed haptic feedback shoes uning vibration motor array for visually impaired people. This project was for the Student Innovation Challenge of the 2nd Korea Haptics Conference, and we took the first place! In the project I mainly worked for designing vibration array.

Bottle holder for hemiplegia patientsMar 2024 - June 2024

Development of screw fastening strategies for robot arm Apr 2021 - June 2022

Under misaligned situation, robot arm cannot fasten screws but humans can do. So, With Force-Torque data from 6-axis sensor attached to screwdriver, I build strategies on how to get over misaliged situations for robot operation.
